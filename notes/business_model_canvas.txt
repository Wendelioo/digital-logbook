COST vs REVENUE ANALYSIS
================================================================================
ASSUMPTIONS (Example: Mid-size Institution):
• 500 students
• 50 teachers
• 5 lab administrators
• 10 working-students
• 3 PC laboratories with 50 workstations each

ESTIMATED ANNUAL REVENUE:
• Base License: $3,000 - $5,000/year
• Support Package: $500 - $1,000/year
• Total: ~$4,000 - $6,000/year per institution

ESTIMATED ANNUAL COSTS:
• Development (10 hours/week @ $50/hr): $26,000/year
• Server hosting: $2,000 - $3,000/year
• Database backup/infrastructure: $1,000 - $2,000/year
• Admin support (20 hours/month @ $25/hr): $6,000/year
• Total: ~$35,000 - $37,000/year

BREAK-EVEN POINT:
• Need 6-10 institutional customers to break even
• Scalable after break-even due to minimal marginal costs

================================================================================
                    FUNCTIONAL DECOMPOSITION DIAGRAM (FDD)
                         Digital Logbook System
================================================================================

A Functional Decomposition Diagram (FDD) is a top-down diagram that breaks
a complex system into smaller, manageable functions, showing how each part
contributes to the whole system.

Project: PC Laboratory Attendance Tracking System
Platform: Wails Desktop Application (Go + React)
